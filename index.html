Tiger Sort Trails Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 20, 2026

Tiger Sort Trails is a casual sorting puzzle game developed for Android. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information when you use it.

App Information

App Name: Tiger Sort Trails

Package Name: com.jadepaw.tigersorttrails

Contact Email: avenix.store.dev@avenix.store

Information We Collect

Tiger Sort Trails does not require users to create an account and does not ask for personally identifiable information such as name, phone number, address, or email address.

The app may store gameplay data locally on your device, including:

  • Coins, stars, hints, and reward status
  • Level progress and level star ratings
  • Game settings such as music and sound preferences

This data is stored locally on your device to keep your game progress and improve your play experience. It is not transmitted to our servers.

How We Use Information

  • Save your game progress
  • Remember your settings
  • Unlock levels, rewards, and collection items
  • Provide a smoother gameplay experience

Third-Party Services

Tiger Sort Trails currently does not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, payment SDKs, or social login services.

If third-party services are added in a future version, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

Because the app does not collect personal information or send gameplay progress to external servers, there is no personal user data shared externally by the app.

Children’s Privacy

Tiger Sort Trails is designed as a casual puzzle game for general audiences. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us at avenix.store.dev@avenix.store and we will take appropriate action.

Data Storage and Deletion

Gameplay progress and settings are stored locally on your device. You can delete this data by clearing the app data from your Android system settings or uninstalling the app.

Security

We use reasonable measures to protect the app experience. Since Tiger Sort Trails does not transmit personal gameplay data to external servers, most game data remains under your control on your own device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the effective date at the top of this document.
